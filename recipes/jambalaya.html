Jambalaya Recipe

Description

Jambalaya is a Lousiana rice dish made up of vegatables, creole seasoning and sausage.

Recipe

  • Andouille sausage or Polska kielbasa
  • chicken
  • flour
  • butter
  • onion
  • green onion
  • garlic
  • celery
  • bell pepper
  • dried basil
  • cajun seasoning
  • diced tomatoes
  • sugar
  • chicken broth
  • long grain white rice

Steps

  1. Brown the sausage and chicken in a large skillet.
  2. Remove the meat to a plate and add butter and flour to the pan.
  3. Add onions, garlic, celery, and bell pepper and saute.
  4. Add basil, cajun seasoning, diced tomatoes, and sugar.
  5. Add chicken broth, rice, and meat back to the pan.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, cover and simmer for 25 minutes.
  7. Allow to rest, covered, for 10 minutes before fluffing with a fork and serving.
